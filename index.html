How has the commerical airline industry been impacted by COVID-19?

Introduction

As all of you sitting and reading this are well aware, the global pandemic of COVID-19 has caused all sorts of wide ranging impacts. Today, I want to examine the specific impact that the virus has had on the economy of US domestic travel. To do this, I collected data from 30 major airport within the United States and calculated the percentage of change in domestic passengers for each month as compared with the same month one calendar year earlier (change between Jan 2019 and Jan 2020 would show up as Jan 2020 percent change). Given the seasonal and cyclical nature of travel, these month to month comparison help provide consistant data and give insignt as to how the industry is looking compared to this time last year.
